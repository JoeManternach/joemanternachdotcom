About Me

Microcontrollers? Oh, they're my jam! Picture this: the euphoria of writing pristine code that runs like a dream, blended with the sheer artistry of elegant circuitry. Put 'em together, and boom! You've got yourself a microcontroller. It's like a symphony of creativity and problem-solving, all rolled into one tiny, electrifying package. And let me tell you, I'm all about that constant quest for knowledge and the thrill of bringing ideas to life. So, when I stumbled upon the Arduino, it was like stumbling upon a gold mine of possibilities. Tired of the same old circuit conundrums in class, I decided to shake things up. Cue the summer of 2023, where I took the plunge and snagged myself an Arduino. And let me tell you, that summer? It was bound to be legendary.
