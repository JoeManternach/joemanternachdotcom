About Me

Microcontrollers? Oh, they're my jam! Picture this: the euphoria of writing pristine code that runs like a dream, blended with the sheer artistry of elegant circuitry.

That my friend is a microcontroller. A symphony of creativity and problem-solving, all rolled into one tiny, electrifying package.

Let me tell you, I'm all about that constant quest for knowledge and the thrill of bringing ideas to life. So, when I stumbled upon the Arduino, it was like stumbling upon a gold mine of possibilities. I was tired of the same old circuit conundrums in class, so I decided to shake things up in the summer of 2023, where I took the plunge and snagged myself an Arduino. And let me tell you, that summer? It was bound to be legendary.
